About

About

An opulent retreat nestled in the heart of the city, Velvet Crown redefines luxury with its timeless elegance and modern allure. Overlooking breathtaking cityscapes and tranquil waters, our hotel offers an unparalleled sanctuary for discerning travelers.

  • 350 lavish rooms, suites, and residences with panoramic views
  • 6 exceptional dining venues, including a rooftop bar with skyline views
  • Exclusive access to a premier shopping and entertainment hub

Established in 1985, Velvet Crown has been a beacon of luxury and tradition for over three decades. Our iconic architecture blends European sophistication with contemporary design, creating a unique and welcoming atmosphere.

Guests have included world leaders, celebrities, and cultural icons—all drawn by the unmatched service, exclusive experiences, and curated events that define our legacy. From wellness retreats to bespoke city tours, Velvet Crown continues to set the gold standard in urban hospitality.

Join us in celebrating timeless hospitality, where every stay becomes a cherished memory.
