About

An opulent retreat nestled in the heart of the city, Velvet Crown redefines luxury with its timeless elegance and modern allure. Overlooking breathtaking cityscapes and tranquil waters, our hotel offers an unparalleled sanctuary for discerning travelers. Unwind in our spacious, exquisitely designed rooms, savor world-class cuisine at our signature restaurants, indulge in curated experiences, or host a flawless event in our grand banquet halls. Whether for business or leisure, Velvet Crown is where sophistication meets comfort.
